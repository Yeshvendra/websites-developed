About Us

Our Clinic offers top notch doctor support.

Dr. S.K. Singh did his M.B.B.S. in 1986 followed by M.S. in Gen. Surgery in 1991 from Jwahar Lal Nehru Medical A.M.U. Aligarh. He is registered with state medical council of UP M.B.B.S. Reg. No. 32399 and M.S. Reg. No. 4983.

He has worked as a Surgoen in Pt. Kamlapati Tripathi Government Hospital(Chandauli) from 1993 to 1997. He joined Shri. Shiv Prasad Gupta Government Hospital(Varanasi) as a Surgeon for a tenure of 7 years from 1997 to 2004. Worked as a Specialist Surgeon in Ministry of Health Saudia Arabia, Ministry of Health Libya, T.H.P.A. project Hospital Gedu Bhutan and P.H.P.A. project Hospital Wangdue Bhutan.

Trained in Endoscopy at Global Open University, World Laparoscopy Hospital, New Delhi. He accomplised upper G.I. Endoscopy at Jigme Dorji Wangchuk National Refferal Hopital, Thimpu, Bhutan.Trained in Sonography at Dept. of ultrasonography, Anand Loke Sonoscan Centre.Color Doppler training at Genesis Scans, Chennai and Adult Echocardiography at Institute of Ultrasound affiliated to Jeffersons University Hospital Philadelphia, U.S. (W.H.O. Recognised Centre). Proficent in Non-Scalpel Vasectomy.

Life Member of Association of Surgeons of India and Indian Medical Association.
